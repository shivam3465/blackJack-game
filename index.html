How to play BlackJack Game 

        
    
        
          
  • First of all you should click on "START GAME" button, this will give you two cards randomly.
    • 

          
  • Now if the sum of the digits of cards is less than 21 then you can add 
            a new card by clicking "NEW CARD" button, and this will be also given to you randomly.
    • 

          
  • Now again if the sum of all the three cards is less than 21 , then again you can add a card as suggest in
            step 2.
    • 

          
  • You can keep adding new cards until your sum either gets equal to 21 OR becomes greater than 21.
    • 

          
  • So if your sum of the cards is equal to 21 then Wohooo! You have got a blackJack! that means you have won the game. 
          
    • 

          
  • And if your sum is greater than 21 then you have lost the game and you are out of it .
    • 

          
  • Now you can play again by clicking the "RESTART" button
    •
